# 📊 Retail Sales Executive Dashboard (Qlik Sense)

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Retail Sales Executive Dashboard** is an interactive Business Intelligence dashboard developed using **Qlik Sense** to analyze retail sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and regional trends. The dashboard enables business users to explore data dynamically through interactive visualizations and filters, helping drive data-informed decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Monitor overall business performance through key KPIs.
- Analyze sales and profit across different cities.
- Identify top-performing product categories.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior.
- Track revenue and profit trends over time.
- Enable interactive exploration of retail data.

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### Executive Summary

The dashboard provides an overview of the business using KPI cards that display:

- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Average Revenue per Order

These KPIs help executives quickly evaluate overall business performance.

---

### Sales Trend Analysis

Visualizes revenue trends over time to identify:

- Sales growth
- Seasonal patterns
- Peak sales periods
- Business trends

This analysis supports forecasting and strategic planning.

---

### City-wise Sales Performance

Compare retail performance across multiple cities:

- Mumbai
- Pune
- Bangalore
- Delhi

This visualization highlights regional performance and growth opportunities.

---

### Category-wise Sales Distribution

Analyze revenue contribution by product category.

Categories include:

- Electronics
- Fashion
- Accessories

The dashboard clearly shows Electronics as the highest revenue-generating category.

---

### Revenue vs Profit Analysis

Compare revenue and profit for each category to evaluate profitability.

This analysis helps identify:

- High-profit products
- Best-performing categories
- Profit margins
- Business opportunities

---

### Customer Analysis

Analyze revenue generated by different customer segments.

Customer Types:

- Premium Customers
- Regular Customers

This visualization helps understand customer value and purchasing patterns.

---

### Top Products by Revenue

The dashboard identifies the highest revenue-generating products, including:

- Laptop
- Mobile
- Tablet
- Shoes
- Others

This insight supports inventory planning and marketing strategies.

---

## 🎛 Interactive Filters

Qlik Sense provides powerful associative filtering, allowing users to explore data interactively.

Available filters include:

- City
- Product Category
- Date

Users can dynamically analyze business performance across different dimensions.
