# 🛍️ RetailPulse Analytics Dashboard (Qlik Sense)

## 📌 Project Overview

**RetailPulse Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Business Intelligence dashboard developed using **Qlik Sense** to provide comprehensive insights into retail sales, profitability, customer behavior, and regional performance.

The dashboard enables business users to monitor KPIs, analyze sales trends, compare city-wise performance, evaluate product categories, and identify revenue-driving products through interactive visualizations and filters.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Monitor overall retail business performance.
- Track revenue, profit, and order metrics.
- Analyze sales trends over time.
- Compare sales across different cities.
- Evaluate product category performance.
- Identify top-selling products.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior.
- Enable interactive business analysis using filters.

---

# 📊 Dashboard Pages

## 1️⃣ Sales Overview

The Sales Overview page provides a high-level summary of business performance.

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- 💰 **Total Revenue:** **1.38M**
- 📈 **Total Profit:** **244.7K**
- 🛒 **Total Orders:** **70**
- 💵 **Average Revenue:** **19.72K**

### Visualizations

- Sales Trend Analysis
- City-wise Sales Comparison
- Category Distribution

### Key Insights

- Electronics contributes over **85%** of total revenue.
- Mumbai generates the highest revenue.
- Sales show recurring peak periods indicating seasonal demand.

---

## 2️⃣ Profit Analysis

This page focuses on profitability across product categories and cities.

### Visualizations

- Revenue vs Profit Scatter Plot
- Profit Distribution by City and Category (Treemap)

### Key Insights

- Electronics is the most profitable category.
- Fashion has moderate profitability.
- Accessories contribute the least revenue and profit.
- Mumbai and Pune generate the highest profits.

---

## 3️⃣ Customer Insights

This page analyzes customer segments and product performance.

### Visualizations

- Customer Type Revenue Analysis
- Top 5 Products by Revenue
- Interactive Filters

### Available Filters

- City
- Category
- Date

### Key Insights

- Premium customers generate significantly more revenue than Regular customers.
- Laptop is the highest revenue-generating product.
- Mobile and Tablet are the next strongest-performing products.
